Overview

The main goal of this sport is to maneuver and navigate the raft through rapids or turbulent waters, often times taking you over rocks and drops, without capsizing the raft.

Purpose

ChiChi whitewater rafting specialises on recreational outdoor activities which use an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other body of water. This is often done on whitewater or different degrees of rough water. Dealing with risk is often a part of the experience.

Audience

Basically our target audience are youths ranging from students to working class. We are in business to service a wide range of fun lovers and risk takers. For us, life is all about risk so we take calculated risks that teaches us to face our fears and still experience life. We target 25% market share, serving an average of 1,200 customers.
